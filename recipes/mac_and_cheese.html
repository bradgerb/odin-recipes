Mac and Cheese

Requirements

Prep time - 10 min

Cook time - 45 min

Servings - 4

Description

This is a homemade mac and cheese recipe from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasoning
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Make cheese sauce, pour over noodles
  3. Make the topping, spread over noodles
  4. Bake until golden brown
