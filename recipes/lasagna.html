Lasagna

Requirements

Prep time - 30 min

Cook time - 2 hr 30 min

Servings - 12

Description

Lasagna is a pasta dish with layers of noodles, meat, cheese, and sauce.

This recipe is from "The worlds best lasagna" found on allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Allow to rest
